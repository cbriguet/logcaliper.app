Sizing and scoping of log management and SIEM solutions

Determine the appropriate size and scope of your system designed to collect, analyze, and store log data (log management) and security-related data and events (SIEM). Sizing and scoping refers to the process of determining the appropriate size and scope of a log management or SIEM solution, including the amount of data to be collected, the number of devices to be monitored, and the resources needed to effectively manage and analyze the data.
